About Me.

Hi! My name is Elliot, and I'm currently studying for a Bachelor of Computer Science (Advanced) at The University of Adelaide.

Coding has always intrieged me from a young age, with the source of this inspiration mostly being from watching movies such as the The Matrix and Tron. The main aspect I enjoy about coding is that there is not correct solution to a problem. The mixed emotions you experience throughout solving coding problems is one of a kind. All the way to the satisfaction from solving a LeetCode problem you've been working at for hours, to the frustration from tedious debugging, is why I believe coding is one of the most rewarding jobs in the world.
